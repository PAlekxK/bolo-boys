Bolo Boys are an Atlanta- and Macon-based three-piece live band focused on one thing: creating fun, high-energy nights where people sing along, raise a glass, and stay longer.

Drawing from cowboy country, folk standards, reggae staples, and classic rock favorites, their sets blend throwback hits and original songs through the band's signature "bolo-fied" style — polished yet relaxed, roots-driven, and built to bring people together.

Nigel (vocals, guitar)  ·  John (vocals, guitar)  ·  Paul (vocals, bass)
