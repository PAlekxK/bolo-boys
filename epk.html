Who we are

Bolo Boys are a three-piece live band out of Atlanta and Macon — cowboy country, folk, reggae, classic rock, and throwback pop, all Bolo-fied until the covers and the four originals sound like they came off the same album. The barometer of a good night is simple: people dancing, and a room singing the hooks back.

Paulvocals, electric bass · Atlanta

Touchstones: Tom Petty, Bob Marley, Doc Watson, Cat Stevens. Originals are woven in so seamlessly you won't notice them between Petty and Marley — that's the point.

Sound & setup

Bolo Boys are fully self-contained — we own, bring, set up, and run our own PA and backline, then break it all down. A room that has never hosted live music can host a full band this weekend: no stage, no house system, nothing for your staff to run.

  • Full PA & mixingYamaha MG12XU 12-channel mixer + EV ZLX-12P 1000W mains
  • Stage monitorsso the band hears itself and the mix stays clean
  • Mics, DIs, stands & cablingvocals and instruments, all of it
  • Setup, sound-check & teardownwe run our own sound, start to finish

Right-sized for real rooms — patios, taprooms, shops, markets, and private gatherings around Atlanta and Macon. A detailed input list and stage plot are available on request.
